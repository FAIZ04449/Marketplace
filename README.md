🏪 14-24 Store
A Hostel Marketplace for Instant Essentials
14-24 Store is a hostel-focused peer-to-peer marketplace inspired by services like Instamart. It enables hostel residents to buy and sell essential items within their hostel community, especially during hours when external delivery services are restricted or unavailable.
The platform connects students inside the hostel so that essential items like snacks, stationery, toiletries, and other necessities can be exchanged quickly.
📌 Problem
Students living in hostels frequently face challenges such as:
Delivery services not allowed inside hostel premises
Restrictions on delivery timings
Urgent need for essentials late at night
Lack of a platform to connect with nearby students who may have the required items
As a result, students often struggle to get basic necessities during restricted hours.
💡 Solution
14-24 Store provides a community-driven marketplace where hostel residents can:
List items they want to sell
Browse items available within the hostel
Connect with other students to purchase items instantly
This creates a localized instant marketplace inside the hostel ecosystem, ensuring accessibility to essential items anytime.
✨ Features
🛍 Item Listings
Users can list products they want to sell with price and description.
🔎 Browse Marketplace
Students can explore items posted by other hostel residents.
⚡ Instant Availability
Items are already within the hostel, enabling quick exchanges.
👥 Peer-to-Peer Marketplace
Buyers and sellers interact directly.
🏫 Hostel-Focused Platform
Built specifically for closed student communities.
